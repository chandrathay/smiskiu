About SmiskiU!

Smiskis are adorable, glow-in-the-dark figurines originally created by a Japanese company called Dreams Inc. These curious little creates love to hide in small spaces, bringing joy and a touch of mystery to those who find them.

SmiskiU combines the charm of Smiskis with the student experience at Boston University. The interactive quiz takes you through a series of 10 fun, campus-inspired questions to determine your perfect Smiski counterpart. Once you complete the quiz, you'll receive a personalized result card that showcases your unique SmiskiU character along with its tagline and a brief description of how it relates to your personality.

About the Author!

Hi! I'm Chandra, a student at Boston University majoring in Media Science and minoring in Computer Science. The idea for SmiskiU came from my love of collecting Smiskis and I wanted to create a fun, interactive way for others to learn about and engage with these cute characters. I put my own spin on this idea by creating custom Smiski characters that embody different aspects of the Boston University experience! I hope you enjoy discovering your SmiskiU match as much as I enjoyed creating this project!
